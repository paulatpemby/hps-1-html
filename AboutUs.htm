About Us

We are a Tasmanian design studio, located in Hobart, Tasmania, specialising in working with people who would like to participate in the design process.

Why us?

Our mission is to give you the designer home without the higher cost of an architect. We turn your vision into reality, solve your existing home space, functionality and sunlight problems and inspire your design with creativity.

We want to see you get value for every dollar you spend on your home.

Our aim

To make your design creation an exciting and fun experience. We will work with you in putting your ideas, needs and desires into the plan throughout the design process.

Our experience

We have over 20 years' experience designing a broad range of projects from a simple deck addition to a brand-new 40 square home. We love designing well laid out homes that are functional and stylish, providing as much space and as many features as the budget allows.
