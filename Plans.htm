House plans

Each design is created individually to suit your every need for your dream home, fabulous renovation/extension or investment property.

  • The functionality of the rooms suits your lifestyle.
  • Any views are captured to their fullest.
  • Passive solar design is included to reduce energy costs and to bring the sun and light into your home.
  • The house placement makes the best use of the block.
  • The interior and exterior design style is tailored to reflect your taste.
  • Practical design solutions are provided to cater for your individual needs or problems areas.
  • The design suits your budget.
